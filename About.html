PETER GENE HERNANDEZ
aka BRUNO MARS

Bruno Mars is a world-renowned American singer, songwriter, and record producer, celebrated for his captivating stage presence and remarkable vocal range. Born Peter Gene Hernandez on October 8, 1985, in Honolulu, Hawaii. His mother emigrated from the Philippines to Hawaii, His parents met while performing in a show in which his mother was a Hula dancer and his father played percussion. At the age of two, he was nicknamed "Bruno" by his father because of his resemblance to professional wrestler Bruno Sammartino.

Throughout his career, Bruno Mars has received numerous accolades, including multiple Grammy Awards, and has collaborated with several esteemed artists, solidifying his position as a prominent figure in the contemporary music industry. With his charismatic performances and chart-topping singles, Bruno Mars continues to captivate audiences worldwide, leaving an indelible mark on the music landscape.
